Welcome to Heart of Gold Hostel - Berlin

Thanks to a central location, the Heart of Gold Hostel is the ideal base from which to explore all the beauties that Berlin can offer.Its budget accommodation, the friendliness of a hostel but the professionalism of a hotel, and an interior design inspired by the ‘Hitchhiker’s Guide to the Galaxy’ will make you feel right at home!

Berlin - the place to be

The historic center with many of Berlin’s most interesting sights are within walking distance, and the nightlife district full of bars and clubs is just around the corner. Making new friends from all around the world and meeting real Berliners at the reception will turn your visit much more worthwhile.
